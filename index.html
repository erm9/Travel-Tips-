Cheap Tips for Exploring Paris

Explore the city of Paris, located in the Northern part of France. Known for its rich history, architecture, fashion, museums, and cuisine. The most desired tourist destination can be affordable, with this itinerary.

Plane Ticket/Transportation

The most affordable time to visit Paris is early December, January, and February. This is the off-season due to the weather. Utilize Google flights and Scotts Cheap flights to find the cheapest roundtrip deals.

Navigo's weekly travel passes are $19.85 USD per week. Paris is best explored on foot, you get the best of both worlds when you use Paris Big bus. It's a hop and hop-off bus with a guided tour to all of Paris's popular destinations.

Places to Stay

The biggest cost for trips is usually boarding. Paris has a lot of affordable options from Airbnb rooms to hostels. St Christopher’s Inn Canal Hostel - $32.48 USD a night. A Peaceful location overlooking the Bassin de la Villette, Paris's biggest lake. Options to choose from private rooms or female-only dorms.

Hotel du Champ Mars, is a Parisian dream. This hotel is only 1km away from the Eiffel tower. Starting at $116 USD a night, to stay in the heart of Paris.

Places to Eat

Tip and trick for fine dining, enjoy a Michelin star course meal. The secret key is to go during lunch! Less busy and for half the price. Benoit - 20 rue Saint Martin. Is one of the oldest french bistros in Paris, dating back to 1912. Enjoy an unforgettable three-course meal for only $45.

Be bold try Escargot at L’Escargot Montorgueil - 38 Rue Montorgueil. This Parisian delicacy is a popular hors d'oeuvre. Smothered in butter and herbs, a land snail has never tasted so good. L’Escargot specializes in escargot and frog legs since 1832.

Afforable Attractions

The best way to navigate Paris; buy a city/museum pass. The Paris city pass contains the following and much more:

  • Bateaux Parisiens River Cruise
  • Eiffel Tower Guided Climb
  • Big Bus Hop-On Hop-Off 1-Day Tour
  • French wine tasting at Les Caves du Louvre

    Affordable Countries Travel to: Cambodia

    Cambodia is located in the southeast asian region. Cambodia has a lot of tourist areas such as sandy shorelines with crystal blue oceans. There are historical places like Tuol Sleng, Otres beach, Koh Rong, and Ta Prohm. The best time to visit Cambodia is between November and January because the temperature is lower. There may be a little rain but it is less crowded.

    Plane Ticket/Transportation

    Cambodia has developed in recent years. They have an affordable transportation system. From cabs, trains, buses, and motorbikes. Most of the transportations in Cambodia is cheap and enjoyable. Tuk-tuk is the most used transportation system in Cambodia and it is also very affordable.

    Places to Stay

    Cambodia has different alternatives for hotel boarding, from family-friendly to luxurious. Their hotels are affordable. Cambodia's capital is Phnom Penh. Its atmosphere is energetic, exciting, and inexpensive. Most places provide services, making it easy for visitors to budget activities. Staying at a guest house can be as cheap as $2 USD a day.

    Places to Eat

    All restaurants in Cambodia are inexpensive compared to other countries. You can save money if you prefer to eat from street vendors, or at the night markets in the city.

    Afforable Attractions

    Cambodia is best known for being the home to Angkor Wat. One of the largest religious monuments in the world. Just hop on a tuk-tuk and buy a ticket to one of the temples. Most temples are under $3 USD, or even free. Making site-seeing affordable.

    Chicago, Illinois, would be a great place to add to your must-see travel destinations list. Chicago also provides affordable hotels with rooms to see The Chicago River that separates the beautiful Downtown Chicago. When you vacation here, you should take advantage of all of Chicago's museums and attractions. Purchasing a city pass is a great way to do this because it offers admissions to five attractions for the price of buying one city pass.

    Plane Ticket/Transportation

    Depending on the trip you desire. The best times to travel to Chicago to experience a warmer trip are April through May. Fall in Chicago; another great time to travel there is September through October if you want to participate in the autumn season.

  • You can fly into the O'Hare International Airport.
  • You can ride the train with Amtrack or Metra to the Chicago Union Station.

    There are many hotels all over Downtown Chicago. Depending on your ideal trip, you may want a view of Grant Park from your hotel. You may want a view of the beautiful Chicago river that flows right between busy downtown life. A vacation here offers many great options.

  • Hampton Inn Chicago Downtown West Loop
  • LondonHouse Chicago
  • Hyatt Regency Chicago

    While visiting Chicago, you will get to experience food from all regions. Below is listed a couple of the many different restaurants Chicago has to offer.

  • Superdawg Drive-In
  • Harold's Chicken Shack
  • Virtue Restaurant

    • Afforable Attractions

    While touring Chicago, the city pass would be an excellent investment. Some places included on Chicago's city pass are listed below. 360 Chicago Observation Deck offers a unique experience at the top of the John Hancook building with TILT glass. This specialized glass is an enclosed glass and moveable steel platform from 1000 feet up. The Chicago Skydeck offers a different experience on the 103rd floor of the Willis Tower. From this observation deck on a clear day, you can see Illinois, Indiana, Wisconsin, and Michigan. The Art Institute of Chicago is another great attraction to explore. The Art Institute is one of the oldest and largest art museums in the world. All of these attractions are included in the Chicago city pass.

  • The Skydeck Chicago

    • Discover Bhutan

    Bhutan is one of the smallest countries in the world, located in the eastern Himalayas, sandwiched between India to the South and Tibet in the north. Bhutan follows the policy of high-value and low impact tourism This was accomplished by imposing minimum costs on overseas travelers. During peak seasons, March-May and September – November, the cost is $250 a day per person; during the low seasons, December – February, and June – August, it's $200 per day. A minimum of 3-star accommodation, all meals, a licensed tour guide, all transportation throughout Bhutan, and all entrance fees are included in the daily charge. There is a surcharge for signal individuals extra ($40 per day) and a group of 2 extra ($30 per day)
